The ICAP nursing e-learning platform is supported with funding from the President’s Emergency Plan for AIDS Relief (PEPFAR) and was developed in collaboration with the Columbia Center for New Media Teaching and Learning (CCNMTL). The e-learning platform is designed to support nursing and midwifery education and training. This platform will serve as an online hub for nursing education and training resources to be accessed by nursing education institutions, in-service training programs, and continuing professional development programs. Supporting nursing education and training is strategic for advancing ICAP’s goal of improving the health of families and communities.

Note:

This module covers key concepts and guidelines recommended at the time of publication. Country specific protocols may vary and new updates provided over time. Please consult your local protocols and treatment guidelines.

This module does not replace local training requirements. Please consult your local authority (nursing council or ministry of health) for training and practice requirements.
