Cohort
Patients who started ART in the same month are called a monthly cohort.
ART Start Date
Date that each patient in the cohort was started on ART.
Why Eligible?
Reason the patient was started on ART. The codes for each reason are listed in a box at the bottom of the register and include PG for pregnancy and BF for breastfeeding.
Estimated Date of Delivery
Date that women who initiated ART during pregnancy are expected to deliver and/or date that women who fall pregnant while on ART are expected to deliver. This will help with interpretation of findings if a patient for example decides to go home to deliver.
Follow-up Status
Whether or not the patient has remained on ART in the months after starting ART. The codes for follow-up status are listed in a box at the bottom of the register and include XX for patients on ART and other codes for patients not on ART.
